What contributes to a password's strength?

Length

The greater the length of a password, the higher its security. An effective password ought to consist of a minimum of 10 characters.

Robust

Robust passcodes incorporate a blend of alphabets, numerals, cases, and symbols to create an unpredictable sequence of characters that doesn't mimic words or names.

Unique

A potent password should be distinct for every account to minimize susceptibility in case of a breach.
